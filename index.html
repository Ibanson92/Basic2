About Me

Hello, I’m Ian Banson. I’m a junior front-end developer with a specialty in Html, CSS, Javascript, and React. I was born in London, England and moved to Maryland at the age of 7. I was always into visual art such as drawing and painting but was never quite good at it. I participated in arts such as music and poetry but once I entered high school I stepped aside from the arts to participate in sports such as football, wrestling, and track. I went on to be captain of the varsity wrestling team both my junior and senior year and was captain of the football team my senior year. I received a full football scholarship to Bucknell University but after being there for a year I transfered to Howard University , later on graduating with a degree in Communication and Culture.
While at Howard I was introduced to Html and CSS in a web design class I took as an elective. This class turned out to be one of my favorite classes I took not just at Howard but in my life. Just the idea of creating gets me excited so when I was able to create websites from code I was astonished. After Howard I enrolled into The George Washington University Full Stack Bootcamp learning how to fully develop websites.
